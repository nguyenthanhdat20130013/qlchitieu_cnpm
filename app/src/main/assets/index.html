ADD NEW RECORDS IN A "SNAP

Tracking money is a fairly straightforward process – you just have to write down everything you spend. But if you have ever tried to do it before, you know that it can be very frustrating with the wrong tools. The more difficult it is to enter a transaction, the less likely you are to actually do it! Monefy understands this and has simplified the process for entering transactions to make it quick, easy, and (relatively) painless. So, next time you buy coffee or take a taxi, fill in the expense amount and hit “Add”. That’s it! One click and done. Tracking your money has never been so quick and easy.

USE IT ON MULTIPLE DEVICES

Do you own a phone and a tablet? Or do you want to track expenses with your significant other? Monefy has you covered. You can safely synchronize data between devices using your own Google Drive or Dropbox account. Create or change records, add new categories or delete old ones and they will automatically sync to the other device. Monefy native application is available for all major mobile platforms.

TRACK YOUR MONEY YOUR WAY

Monefy is ready to go right out of the box. First time using Monefy? No problem. We have carefully selected the default categories to help you start tracking important expenses right from the word “go”. No time-consuming setup process. No need to manually type in each category. Feeling the need for some customization? You can easily change the default categories, add new ones, select the currency you want to use and choose the most convenient language for you through Monefy’s intuitive interface.
